PADDING

padding is a property that adds space on the inside of your button. for example "padding-left" adds space on the left side of the button.

VERTICaL ALIGN

When we add padding to our buttons, they get aligned based on the text, i.e. the text present inside the buttons aligns with each other rather than the buttons themselves. to fix this we use a property called vertical-align that aligns the buttons whichever way we like as here subscribe button is aligned to the top.
